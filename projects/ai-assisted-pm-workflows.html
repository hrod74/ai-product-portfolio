AI product practice

AI-Assisted PM Workflows

A working library of prompts, templates, and rituals for turning messy product questions into sharper research, specs, and stakeholder updates.

Tools
ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Loom
Outcome
Faster discovery without losing rigor

Case Study

A practical AI product practice for moving from vague questions to better artifacts and decisions.

Product challenge

Product work often starts messy: unclear questions, partial context, scattered notes, and pressure to move fast. The goal was to create reusable workflows that speed up discovery without replacing human judgment.

Approach

  • Built prompt patterns for research synthesis, strategy framing, and product requirement drafts.
  • Created lightweight rituals for reviewing AI output against customer evidence and business goals.
  • Used rapid prototyping tools to make ideas concrete earlier in the decision process.

What it shows

This project shows how AI can support product leadership when it is used to sharpen thinking, accelerate artifacts, and make tradeoffs easier to discuss.
