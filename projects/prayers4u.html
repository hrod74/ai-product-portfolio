Mobile rebuild

Prayers4U

Reworked onboarding, information architecture, and community flow for a reverent mobile experience that helps people post, find, and respond with care.

Tools
Figma, mobile UX, testing
Outcome
Cleaner path from intent to action

Case Study

A mobile experience shaped around clarity, care, and respectful community participation.

Product challenge

The product needed to support a sensitive user intent without becoming confusing, cluttered, or transactional. The experience had to make posting, finding, and responding feel simple while preserving the tone of the community.

Approach

  • Reviewed onboarding and first-action moments to reduce hesitation.
  • Reworked the information architecture around user intent and community flow.
  • Focused the mobile interface on clear choices, readable hierarchy, and thoughtful interaction states.

What it shows

This project highlights mobile product judgment, user empathy, and the ability to make complex emotional workflows feel grounded and usable.
