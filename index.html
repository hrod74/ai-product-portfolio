About

A product leader who likes the hard middle: customer truth, business pressure, and team reality.

I have spent my career building products where the details matter: marketplace liquidity, consumer conversion, trust, workflow clarity, and the small moments that make a customer feel the product was built by people paying attention.

My current work centers on AI as a practical product advantage. Used well, it helps teams learn faster, write better specs, pressure-test decisions, and get prototypes in front of people sooner. The craft is knowing when the tool is accelerating insight and when it is only adding noise.
